# Zhangye

## Telemetry

- Terrain: An oasis city in the middle section of the Hexi Corridor, with the Qilian Mountains to the south and a mix of alluvial plains, Heihe wetlands, and Danxia landforms nearby.
- Step: Located on China’s second terrain step, at the transition between the northeastern edge of the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau and the inland arid zone of the Hexi Corridor.
- Climate: Temperate continental arid climate with strong sunshine, limited precipitation, high evaporation, and a clear day-night temperature range.
- Challenge: Gobi winds, strong solar exposure, and long-distance resupply place higher demands on vehicle endurance, thermal management, and field protection.
