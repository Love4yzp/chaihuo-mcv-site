# Ruoqiang

## Telemetry

- Terrain: Southeastern edge of the Tarim Basin, transition zone below the Altyn-Tagh Mountains
- Step: Second Terrain Step
- Climate: Warm temperate continental extremely arid climate
- Challenge: Temperature swings, dust, and continuous transfer pressure after descending from the plateau

## Activities

- Xinjiang entry transfer point
- Basin-edge environment logging

## Event

The route entered Xinjiang from Mangya, with Ruoqiang marking the transition from plateau desert to the Tarim Basin edge.
