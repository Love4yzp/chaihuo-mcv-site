## Event

From Shihezi, the MCV headed north to Karamay, continuing westward along the northern slope of the Tianshan range into the heart of the Junggar Basin. This marks the site of China's first major oil field — a landscape where oil fields and desert interweave, offering a unique new observation point on the northern Xinjiang route.
